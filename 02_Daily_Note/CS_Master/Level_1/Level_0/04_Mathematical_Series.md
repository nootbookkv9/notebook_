## Mathematical Series =====================================

============================================================
# Arithmetic Series
  - 1+2=3 , 3+2=5 , 5+2=7 , ...
  - 1,3,5,7,...

# Geometric Series
  - 1*2=2 , 2*2=4 , 4*2=8 , ...
  - 1,2,4,8,16,...

============================================================

# SUM OF TERMS
  - Arithmetic Series
      1+2+3+...n = n((n+1) / 2)

      1+2+3+4+5  = 5((5+1) / 2)
      1+2+3+4+5  = 5((6) / 2)
      1+2+3+4+5  = 5(3)
      1+2+3+4+5  = 15

  - Geometric Series
      n((1-r**n) / 1-r) --> Note: (r != 1)
