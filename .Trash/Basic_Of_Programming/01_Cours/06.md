# How to convert from Decimal to Hexadecimal
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Number / 16    | Result         | Integer        | Fraction       | Remander          | Hex            |
|----------------|----------------|----------------|----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| 1513   / 16    | 94.5625        | 94             | 0.5625         | 16 * 0.5625 =  9  | 9              |
|   94   / 16    |  5.875         |  5             | 0.875          | 16 * 0.875  = 14  | E              |
|    5   / 16    |  -.-           |  -             | -.-            |             =  5  | 5              |
